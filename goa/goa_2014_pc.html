GOA IN THE 2014 NATIONAL ELECTION

This gives us a look at the results of the last national election in 2014 broken down by seats in the state legislative election. The BJP (Bharatiya Janta Party) would have won a clear majority with 33 out of 40 seats. The INC (Indian National Congress) would have won the other seven, with seats in Bardez in North Goa and Salcette in South Goa.

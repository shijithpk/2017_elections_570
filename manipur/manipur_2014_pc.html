MANIPUR IN THE 2014 NATIONAL ELECTION

If the results of the 2014 national election are broken down by state legislative seats, the INC (Indian National Congress) would win a majority with 38 seats. The NPF (Naga Peoples Front) would win 9 seats in Tamenglong to the east, Senapati and Ukhrul to the north and Chandel to the south.

MANIPUR IN THE 2012 STATE ELECTION

The INC (Indian National Congress) won a majority in the last Manipur state election in 2012. It won 42 out of 60 seats with other seats going to regional parties such as the NPF (Naga Peoples Front). The NPF won four seats in the northern regions of Senapati and Ukhrul and the southern region of Chandel.
